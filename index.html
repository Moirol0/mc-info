UNDERSTANDING REPS IN RESERVE

RIR tracks how many clean reps you have left in the tank before hitting absolute failure.

* 2 RIR: Stop when you could only do 2 more clean reps. Perfect for heavy strength work without frying your nervous system.

* 1 RIR: Pushing right next to the edge. Ideal for targeting muscle hypertrophy.

* 0 RIR: Complete physical failure. Your form is totally breaking down.
